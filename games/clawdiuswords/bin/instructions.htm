Meet Clawdius!

Clawdius is a friendly crab who wants to help reconstruct W&M! He will sit under your game grid for moral support

What to do

Clawdius needs help after a rough day. he was swimming College Creek and now can't seem to remember his favorite words

You are able to guess Clawdius' words 6 times. Similar to the NYT's Wordle game, the letters change color based on whether they are in the word and the correct position

Good luck!
